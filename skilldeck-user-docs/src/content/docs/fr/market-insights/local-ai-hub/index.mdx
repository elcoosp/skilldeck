The future of AI is **local**. This hub collects articles, case studies, and comparisons explaining why running AI on your own hardware is becoming the preferred choice for developers who care about privacy, cost, and control.

## Why Local AI?

### 🔒 Privacy by Design
When you use cloud AI services, your code, prompts, and data are sent to third‑party servers. With local AI, everything stays on your machine. No data leaves your control, no training on your proprietary code, no compliance headaches.

### 💰 Predictable Costs
Cloud AI bills vary with usage – a single project can generate unexpected costs. Local AI runs on hardware you already own, with no per‑token fees. Once you've set it up, the marginal cost of each request is near zero.

### ⚡ Speed and Reliability
Local models eliminate network latency. Responses are instant, and you're never at the mercy of an external API's uptime or rate limits. Your AI works offline, anywhere.

### 🔧 Full Control
You choose which models to run, how to configure them, and when to update. No vendor lock‑in, no sudden deprecations, no policy changes that break your workflows.
