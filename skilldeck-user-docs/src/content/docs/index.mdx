## Why SkillDeck?

SkillDeck is designed for developers who need AI that understands their code—without compromising security or control. Your data stays on your machine, your skills are version‑controlled like code, and your team shares knowledge that compounds over time.

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Local‑First by Default" icon="key">
    Your code never leaves your machine. API keys live in your OS keychain, conversations stay local.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Skills as Code" icon="puzzle">
    Version‑controlled, sharable as Gists, and composable like any other dev tool. Install from our registry or create your own.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Agentic Workflows" icon="rocket">
    From simple chat to complex parallel orchestrations. Use sequential, parallel, or evaluator‑optimizer patterns.
  </Card>
  <Card title="MCP Ecosystem" icon="link">
    Connect to any tool via the Model Context Protocol—filesystem, GitHub, Slack, databases, and more.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
