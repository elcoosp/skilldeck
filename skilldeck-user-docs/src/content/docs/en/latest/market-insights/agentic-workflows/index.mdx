Agentic workflows are the next evolution of AI assistance – moving from single, isolated prompts to coordinated, multi‑step processes. This hub covers everything you need to design, build, and optimize workflows in SkillDeck.

## What Are Agentic Workflows?

An agentic workflow is a sequence of steps executed by one or more AI agents to accomplish a complex task. Each step can invoke skills, use MCP tools, or even spawn sub‑agents. Workflows bring structure, repeatability, and reliability to AI‑driven processes.

## Why Workflows Matter

- **Consistency**: Define a process once, run it repeatedly.
- **Reliability**: Break complex tasks into verifiable steps.
- **Auditability**: Each step can be logged and reviewed.
- **Composability**: Mix and match skills to create new capabilities.
