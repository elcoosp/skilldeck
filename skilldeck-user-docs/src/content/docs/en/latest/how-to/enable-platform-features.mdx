SkillDeck Platform is an optional service that adds community features to your local installation. By enabling it, you can browse the skill registry, receive helpful nudges, and earn referral rewards.

<Nudge type="tip" title="What you'll learn">
  How to connect your local SkillDeck to the platform and manage your preferences.
</Nudge>

## Prerequisites

- SkillDeck installed and running
- (Optional) An email address for notifications

## Step 1: Open Platform Settings

1. Click the **Settings** icon (gear) in the left sidebar.
2. Navigate to the **Platform** tab.

## Step 2: Enable Platform Sync

1. Toggle **Enable platform sync** to the ON position.
2. The default platform URL is `https://platform.skilldeck.dev`. If you're using a self‑hosted instance, change it to your custom URL.
3. Click **Save Settings**.

SkillDeck will now attempt to register your installation with the platform.

<Checkpoint question="Did you see a success message?" onYes="Great! Your installation is now connected." onNo="Check your internet connection and try again. If using a custom URL, ensure it's correct." />

## Step 3: (Optional) Add Your Email

Adding your email allows you to receive nudge notifications and referral rewards.

1. In the same **Platform** tab, find the **Email** section.
2. Enter your email address and click **Save**.
3. You'll receive a verification email – click the link to confirm.

<Nudge type="tip" title="Privacy">
  Your email is stored securely and only used for platform communications. You can opt out anytime.
</Nudge>

<Checkpoint question="Did you verify your email?" onYes="Perfect! You'll now receive nudges and can participate in referrals." onNo="Check your spam folder and click the verification link." />

## Step 4: Adjust Nudge Frequency

Nudges are helpful tips and reminders delivered via in‑app toasts or email (if you've added email). You can control how often you receive them:

- **Daily** – a nudge every day (can be frequent)
- **Weekly** – one nudge per week
- **Important only** – only when there's critical information (e.g., security updates)

Choose your preference in the **Nudge Frequency** dropdown.

## Step 5: Explore the Skill Registry

With platform sync enabled, the **Skills** tab now shows community skills from the registry. You can browse, search, and install them just like local skills.

<Checkpoint question="Can you see community skills in the Skills tab?" onYes="Awesome! You're now part of the SkillDeck ecosystem." onNo="Try refreshing the Skills tab or restarting SkillDeck." />

## Referral Program

When you refer other developers to SkillDeck, you can earn rewards. To get started:

1. In the **Platform** tab, click the **Referral** subtab (or go to **Settings → Refer & Earn**).
2. Click **Create my referral link**.
3. Share your unique link with colleagues.

You'll see stats on signups, conversions, and rewards earned.

## Disabling Platform Features

If you ever want to go back to a fully local setup:

1. Go to **Settings → Platform**.
2. Toggle **Enable platform sync** OFF.
3. Click **Save Settings**.

Your local data (conversations, skills, profiles) remains untouched. Only future platform interactions stop.
