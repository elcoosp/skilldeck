The Community Spotlight is our way of recognizing awesome members of the SkillDeck community. If you know someone who deserves recognition – for their contributions, skills, or helpfulness – use this template to nominate them.

## Nomination Guidelines

- **Nominees can be anyone**: Contributors, power users, skill authors, or active community members.
- **Self-nominations are welcome**: Proud of your work? Tell us about it!
- **Spotlights are published monthly**: We feature one person per month.
- **Submissions are reviewed by the core team**: We may edit for clarity and length.

## Template

Copy and paste the following into a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/elcoosp/skilldeck/issues/new) or email to [community@skilldeck.dev](mailto:community@skilldeck.dev).

```markdown
### Nominee Information

**Name:**
**GitHub handle:**
**Discord username (optional):**
**How long have they been part of the SkillDeck community?**

### Contribution Highlights

What have they done that makes them stand out? (e.g., built popular skills, helped many users, contributed code/documentation, etc.)

### Skills They've Created (if any)

List any skills they've authored, with links if available.

### Why They Deserve the Spotlight

Tell us why you're nominating them. Include specific examples if possible.

### Anything Else?

Is there anything else you'd like to share? (Fun facts, projects they're working on, etc.)

### Your Info (optional, if we need to follow up)

**Your name:**
**Your contact (email/Discord):**
```
