SkillDeck Platform is an optional cloud service that adds community features to your local installation. You can enable it in Settings → Platform.

## Core Features

### Skill Registry

The registry is a curated marketplace of community‑contributed skills. When platform sync is enabled, the Skills tab shows both local and registry skills. You can browse, search, and install registry skills with one click.

**Benefits:**
- Discover skills you didn't know existed.
- Install community‑tested skills quickly.
- Contribute your own skills (via GitHub Gist) for others to use.

### Nudges

Nudges are helpful tips and reminders delivered in‑app (toast notifications) and optionally via email. They help you get the most out of SkillDeck.

**Types of nudges:**
- **Feature reminders**: "Did you know you can attach folders with # ?"
- **Best practices**: "Consider adding a license to your skill."
- **Community updates**: "New skills added to the registry!"

You can control frequency (Daily, Weekly, Important Only) or opt out entirely.

### Referrals

Share SkillDeck with your colleagues and earn rewards. When you refer someone who signs up and becomes active, you both benefit.

**How it works:**
1. Create a referral link in Settings → Platform → Referrals.
2. Share the link with a friend.
3. When they install SkillDeck and enable platform features, you get credit.

Rewards may include early access to new features, swag, or other perks.

## Privacy and Data

Platform features are opt‑in. When disabled, SkillDeck remains fully local – no data leaves your machine. When enabled:

- Your email is stored only for nudge delivery and referral tracking.
- Anonymous usage data may be collected to improve the registry (you can opt out in Settings).
- No conversation data or code is ever sent to the platform.
